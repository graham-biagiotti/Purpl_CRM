purpl

Lavender lemonade that earns its shelf space

purpl is a lavender-infused sparkling lemonade available in four flavors — Classic, Blueberry, Peach, and Raspberry. MSRP $3.29 per can. NH-made, clean-ingredient, and built for specialty grocery, co-ops, cafes, and wellness retail.

Classic
Clean citrus, soft lavender finish. Bright and lightly floral, never overpowering.
Blueberry
Wild blueberry and lavender. Tart, sweet, and unmistakably New England.
Peach
Sun-ripe peach with a lavender lift. Smooth and effortlessly refreshing.
Raspberry
Bold raspberry with a floral finish. Bright and a little unexpected.
Real Lavender. Real Farm.

Pumpkin Blossom Farm sits on nearly 14 acres in Warner, New Hampshire — home to over 10,000 lavender plants across four varieties: Phenomenal, Sensational, Exceptional, and Big Ti. A family-owned farm since 2007, grown without pesticides on a sun-drenched hillside.

The farm is where Lavender Fields was born. It is the story behind both brands and the reason lavender is at the center of everything we make.

Our story

Pumpkin Blossom Farm is a family-owned lavender farm on nearly 14 acres in Warner, New Hampshire. The property has been the center of our family's life since 2007 and the home of Lavender Fields, our handcrafted lavender goods line.

purpl is our foray into the beverage world — a lavender-infused sparkling lemonade made in New Hampshire, built for the specialty retail shelf.
