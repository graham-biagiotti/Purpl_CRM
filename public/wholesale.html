purpl

Lavender lemonade that earns its shelf space

purpl is a lavender-infused sparkling lemonade available in five flavors — Classic, Blueberry, Peach, Raspberry, and a Variety 12-pack. Sold in 12-pack cases at an MSRP of $3.29 per can, purpl sits at the intersection of the functional beverage and specialty lemonade categories. It is NH-made, clean-ingredient, and built for specialty grocery, co-ops, cafes, and wellness retail.

Lavender Fields

Handcrafted lavender goods from our fields

Lavender Fields at Pumpkin Blossom Farm is home to over 7,000 lavender plants grown without pesticides in Warner, New Hampshire. We handcraft a range of self-care and culinary products using our farm-grown lavender — from simple syrup to aromatherapy accessories. Woman-owned, NH-made, and designed for specialty, gift, spa, and wellness retail.
