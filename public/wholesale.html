Our story

Born from the farm.
Built for the shelf.

Pumpkin Blossom Farm is a family-owned, woman-owned lavender farm on nearly 14 acres in Warner, New Hampshire — home to over 10,000 lavender plants and recognized as one of the largest lavender growers in New England.

The same proven expertise that built our award-winning Lavender Fields wholesale program now powers purpl — crafted for scale, made in New Hampshire.

Lavender Fields

Handcrafted lavender goods from our fields

Lavender Fields at Pumpkin Blossom Farm is home to over 7,000 lavender plants grown without pesticides in Warner, New Hampshire. We handcraft a range of self-care and culinary products using our farm-grown lavender — from simple syrup to aromatherapy accessories. Woman-owned, NH-made, and designed for specialty, gift, spa, and wellness retail.
