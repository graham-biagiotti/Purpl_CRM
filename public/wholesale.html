Our Story

From the farm.

purpl began on a small lavender farm in Warner, New Hampshire. What started as a farm-stand favorite became something bigger — a beverage brand built to bring those calm farm moments into everyday life.

We are Pumpkin Blossom Farm. We grow the lavender, we craft the product, and we personally bring it to your shelf. Every wholesale account is a relationship we take seriously.
